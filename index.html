    What We Plan To Do?

    Our Sylhet Aid team will handout relief packages to affect homes which includes food packs and boats for essential travelling. We plan to help the most vulnerable people to access nutritious food and essential items as soon as possible.

    What Difference Will It Make?

    Our intentions are to provide Emergency Aid to those in need and help the flood victims of Bangladesh with food packs and essential items.
